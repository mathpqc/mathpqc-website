Mathematics for Post-Quantum Cryptography (MathPQC 2026)

MathPQC 2026 is a one-day affiliated workshop held during ASIACRYPT 2026. It connects mathematicians and cryptographers to explore the mathematical foundations of post-quantum cryptography.

Date: Monday, December 7, 2026

Venue: Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong

Format: Whole-day workshop with invited talks and contributed talks

Post-quantum schemes rely on several families of hard mathematical problems. This event focuses on the mathematical foundations of these schemes, including lattice problems, isogenies between supersingular elliptic curves, decoding problems for linear codes, and multivariate quadratic equations over finite fields.

MathPQC 2026 brings together experts in mathematics and cryptography to exchange ideas across disciplines, showcase recent developments, and build new collaborations.
